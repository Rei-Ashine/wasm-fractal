Mandelbrot Set

A complex number \( c \)
is in the Mandelbrot set if,
as \( i \) → \( ∞ \), \( z_i \) does not
diverge where :

\[ z_i = \begin{cases} \space z_{i-1}^2 + \space c & (i \geqq 1) \\ \\[0.01em] \space 0 & (i = 0) \end{cases} \]
